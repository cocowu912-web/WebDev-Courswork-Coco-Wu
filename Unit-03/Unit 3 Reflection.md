# Unit 3 Reflection

[Reflect on your journey here]
In this unit, I learned about the Design Thinking Process and how to work with a client. I chose Kai as my client because I like art and nature too.
The most important step was Empathize. I read Kai's interview and learned that he is an environmental artist who loves blue and green. He wants to use his website to show his art and tell people to protect the planet. His core need is to solve environmental apathy.
Writing the define statement was a bit difficult at first. But after reading the example, I understood that I need to focus on what the user needs, not what I want. Kai needs a way to share his environmental art so as to inspire people to care about nature.
For the Ideate step, I thought about what tags to use. I used <strong> for important words like "passionate" and "planet". I used <em> for artistic words. I used <mark> to highlight "Arizona", "awards", and "global warming" because they are key facts about Kai.
I did not use CSS because we have not learned it yet. The page looks simple but the content shows who Kai is.
