How You Can Help

Everyone can make a difference. Here are ways to save water and help others:

At Home

  • Fix leaks quickly
  • Use water-saving shower heads
  • Collect rainwater for plants

In the Community

  • Volunteer at local water projects
  • Donate to clean water charities

  • Join our monthly clean-up events
