# Unit 2 Reflection

[Reflect on your journey here]
In this unit, I learned about web design and the Design Thinking Process. Before this, I thought design was only about making things look beautiful. Now I know that good design also needs to be functional and easy to use.
The most useful thing I learned was the difference between UI and UX. UI is about how a website looks, while UX is about how people feel when they use it. Both are important because a pretty website can still be difficult to use.
I also learned about the five stages of Design Thinking: Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype, and Test. I think Empathize is the most important stage because we must understand users' needs first. Storytelling is also interesting because it helps designers share ideas with others more clearly.
Overall, this unit changed my idea about design. I now know it is not just art, but also science and technology.
