Our Community Need

The community needs better access to clean water in order to improve health and education for all families.

What We Found

  1. Old pipes leak and waste water
  2. Some areas have no water filters
  3. People do not know how to save water
  4. Schools need clean water for students

Our Goals

Short Term
Fix broken pipes and install filters
Medium Term
Teach families about water safety
Long Term
Build new water stations for everyone

Who We Help

We focus on local schools and low-income families who need help most.
