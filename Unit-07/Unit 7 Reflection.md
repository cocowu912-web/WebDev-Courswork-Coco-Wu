# Unit 7 Reflection

[Reflect on your journey here]
In this unit, I learned about the UN Global Goals and how to create a multi-page website. Before this, I only made single pages. Now I understand how to link different pages together with a navigation bar.
I chose Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation because water is important for everyone. I interviewed a community member and found that old pipes and lack of filters are big problems here. My define statement is: The community needs better access to clean water in order to improve health and education for all families.
The most useful thing I learned was how to make a navigation menu. I used <nav> with <a> links to connect my three pages: Home, About, and Take Action. This helps users move around the site easily.
I also used a <dl> description list to show short, medium, and long term goals. I think this makes the information clear and organized.
One thing I found difficult was choosing colors that match the water theme. I used light blue for the background and darker blue for headings. I hope this gives a clean and fresh feeling to visitors.
Overall, this unit taught me to think about real community problems and use web design to help solve them.
